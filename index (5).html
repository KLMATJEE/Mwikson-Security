Your questions answered
How can I get started with your services?

Simply request a consultation via our online form. We'll schedule a complimentary no-obligation risk assessment tailored to your personal needs or the requirements of your business.

What makes Mwikson Security different?

While many security companies can provide a physical presence, what truly sets Mwikson Security apart is our intelligence-led approach, which is built on a deep and realistic understanding of the security landscape in South Africa. We do not just react to incidents, we proactively work to prevent them.

Why is it essential to protect my home, estate, complex or business?

In today's environment, professional security is no longer a luxury, it is a fundamental necessity. At Mwikson Security, we believe that protecting your property is about much more than just safeguarding physical assets, it is about creating a secure environment where people can live, work, and thrive with confidence and peace of mind.

What is a Threat Analysis?

A Threat Analysis is a professional evaluation to identify the specific security risks your property faces. Instead of guesswork, we systematically assess your property's physical vulnerabilities and analyse local crime intelligence to understand the real-world threats in your area. This allows us to create a smart, proactive security plan tailored to prevent incidents before they happen.

Are security services affordable?

We believe the right question is "Can you afford not to have security?" The potential cost of a single security breach — from financial loss and operational disruption to the irreversible loss of your peace of mind — is almost always far greater than the investment in a professional security plan.
