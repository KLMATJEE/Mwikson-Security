Our Origin & Mission

Founded in Johannesburg in 2019, Mwikson Security (Pty) Ltd was established to bring a new standard of excellence and principle-centered security to the modern business arena.

We are committed to providing the best value for money to our clients, ensuring their peace of mind through robust and reliable security services.

Our mission is to lead the South African security industry by being a service provider that is process-oriented while retaining the flexibility for innovation and creation, always putting our clients' needs and safety first.

Our Vision
